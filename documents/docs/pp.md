# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 17/01/26

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains **what data we collect, how it is used, and how it is handled** when you use or purchase our services.

By purchasing our services, you confirm that you have **read, understood, and accepted** this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree, **do not purchase**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect **only the minimum data required** to provide our service.

### Data we collect directly:
- **Device UDID**  
  Required to process and issue your iDevice certificate
- **Discord User ID and/or Username**  
  Used for identification, support, and recordkeeping within our bot system to provide each user with their own certificate

We do **not** collect names, addresses, IP addresses, phone numbers, or any unnecessary personal information.

---

## 2. Payment Information (Stripe)

All payments are processed by **Stripe**.

We do **not** store:
- Credit card numbers  
- Bank details  
- Billing addresses  

Stripe securely handles all payment data according to its own privacy and security policies.

We may access **basic transaction details from Stripe** (such as payment status, transaction ID, or amount) **only** to:
- Verify your purchase
- Resolve support tickets
- Confirm ownership of an order

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used **only** for:
- Processing and delivering your certificate
- Verifying purchases
- Providing customer support via Discord
- Internal recordkeeping within our bot system

We do **not** use your data for:
- Advertising
- Tracking
- Analytics
- Marketing
- Selling or sharing with third parties

---

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

We store **only**:
- UDID

- Purchase verification references from Stripe

Data is retained **only as long as necessary** for:
- Service delivery

We do **not** intentionally retain unnecessary or excess data.
